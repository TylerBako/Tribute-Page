Short description of his life

Born 18th July 1918, Nelson Mandela also known as the first South African President was an anti-apartheid activist and the first elected in a fully representative democratic election.
Rolihlahla Mandela apart of the Thembu royal family in Mvezo served 27 years in prison starting in 1962 spliting the sentence between Robben Island, Polls,oor Prison and victor verster prison due to conspiring to overthrow the state.
Released in 1990 by president F.W.de Klerk they jointly led all efforts to end the apartheid which ultimatly led to the first multiracial general election within South Africa leading to Mandela's presidency.


  • Nelson Mandela had a presidency length of 5 years from 1994-1999 becoming the first black president of South Africa.
  • He was considered the father of Modern South Africa and was a key driving factor in the downfall of the oppressive government of the time and brought about democray to South Africa.
  • July 18th is now heralded as Nelson Mandela's International Day where the common person should spend at least 67 minutes doing something good for other people to represent the 67 years he spent of his life advocating for change.
  • Mandela spent a total of 27 years in prison
  • Nelson Mandela was born with the name Rolihlahla which means "to pull of a branch tree"
